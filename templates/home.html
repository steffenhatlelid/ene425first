Transport Carbon Emissions Calculator App

Carbon emissions constitute the 60% of the ecological footprint, and are one of the main contributors to climate change.

Transport carbon emissions are an important share of carbon emissions. During the course ENE425 Sustainable Energy and App Development at the Norwegian School of Economics, we design a methodology to compute transport carbon emissions, and we develop an App to track those emissions.

In this web page, I describe that methodology, I deploy an app calculator where any person can work out transport her carbon emissions. I provide a link to the code of the App in my Github account. I also provide the methodoly to work out those emissions. Finally, I introduce a short bio and a link to my curriculum.
